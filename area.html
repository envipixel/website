EnviPixel

Bridging Technology and Nature for a Sustainable Future

EnviPixel Solutions has a highly skilled team ready to operate in five strategic areas: Geotechnology, offering innovative solutions through remote sensing technologies and spatial analysis; Technology, developing cutting-edge tools for process optimization and automation; Forest Quantification, with precise methods for inventory, monitoring, and quantification of forest resources; Environmental, focused on analyzing and managing environmental impacts and promoting sustainable development; and Social and Economic, aiming to integrate solutions that deliver socio-economic benefits by combining technology and sustainability to meet clients' needs with excellence.

We have a team of specialists who can assist in the development of various technological initiatives. We offer ready-made solutions and have a qualified team for the development of new, customized solutions tailored to your business.

We have extensive experience in obtaining and developing geotechnological products. We possess modern equipment that enables the generation of three-dimensional products such as LiDAR point clouds, as well as multi- and hyperspectral equipment. We combine expertise in using the best data processing techniques, including artificial intelligence, to ensure accuracy in the products generated.

We are a company with extensive experience in forest resource quantification. We apply the best techniques for direct and indirect determinations of biomass and carbon, as well as forest volume estimates. We also have broad expertise in floristic surveys of vegetation and studies on classification and successional stages.

We operate in environmental analysis and compliance, always applying the best available techniques by integrating remote sensing, fieldwork, artificial intelligence, and advanced modeling. We provide solutions that enable a reduction in field sampling efforts.

We engage in social and environmental initiatives, developing training, events, and on-demand courses in the fields of remote sensing, as well as environmental and forestry areas. We are equipped to conduct economic and market analyses, price surveys for timber and land. Additionally, we offer a platform to connect stakeholders with shared interests in social projects.
